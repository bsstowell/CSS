Welcome to Biggie B's Memorabilia! Biggie B's Memorabilia was established in 2015, as a dream job that was ultimately fulfilled by me, Brian (Biggie B). Over the years it has become one of the premier sports memorabilia shops in the Midwest, offering thousands items for purchase both in store and on eBay. Biggie B's also sports and celebrity autographed memorabilia from baseball bats and football helmets to photos and toys autographed by a long list of athletes and celebs. Every autograph has been certified by one of the top certification companies including Beckett, Fanatics, James Spence, and PSA/DNA. Biggie B's takes pride in bringing in different and unique items for sale, many of which are obtained from our private signings with the athletes and celebrities present.

Biggie B's is located in the great city of Austin, Texas. Right in the heart of the Rainey Street District, just a couple of turns off of Interstate 35 right off of Lady Bird Lake. We take pride in the fact that we are part of such an incredible atmosphere within the city of Austin. Not only can you stop in for a visit with us, but Austin offers live music all nights of the week, as well as great dine-in experiences.
